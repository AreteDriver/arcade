# Ace Pilot - Signature Character Moments

## Character Profile: "Ace"

**Personality Traits:**
- Confident, skilled pilot who knows their worth
- Frustrated with circumstances but determined to prove themselves
- Develops attachment to the beat-up Rifter despite initial complaints
- Earns respect through actions, not words
- Grows from complaining about equipment to celebrating with it

**Voice:**
- Direct, no-nonsense
- Uses military/pilot slang naturally
- Sarcastic when frustrated, triumphant when victorious
- Shows vulnerability (complaining) and confidence (victory shouts)

### Variations by Context

**EXCELLENT Performance (Flawless or near-flawless):**
- Shout: "IN RUST WE TRUST!"
- Display: Large, bold, golden text with glow effect
- Animation: Text explodes onto screen with particles
- Audio: Triumphant, confident delivery

**GOOD Performance (Solid victory):**
- Shout: "Duct tape holds! In Rust We Trust!"
- Display: Medium text, orange glow
- Animation: Text slides in with ship spark effects
- Audio: Relieved but proud

**SURVIVAL Performance (Barely made it):**
- Shout: "Still flying! In Rust We Trust!"
- Display: Smaller text, dim glow
- Animation: Text appears with engine sputter sounds
- Audio: Exhausted but satisfied

**DIFFICULT (Many deaths/struggles):**
- NO victory shout (too beaten up)
- Elder says: "The ship can be rebuilt. You cannot. Fly safer, Ace."
- Display: Stats only, somber tone

### Evolution Through Progression

**Mission 1-5 (Rifter Only):**
- "In Rust We Trust!" is THE catchphrase
- Becomes associated with underdog victories
- Players start saying it themselves

**First Wolf Mission (After Upgrade):**
- Shout: "No more rust - just steel!"
- Acknowledges growth and change
- Nostalgic callback to Rifter days

**Wolf Missions (Tier 1):**
- "The Wolf hunts well!"
- "Upgraded and deadly!"
- More professional, less scrappy

**First Jaguar Mission (Ultimate Ship):**
- Shout: "From rust to legend!"
- Callbacks to the beginning
- Ultimate payoff for progression

**Jaguar Missions (Tier 2):**
- "Lightning strike! Mission complete!"
- "Jaguar precision!"
- Peak confidence, earned swagger

## Character Arc Through Catchphrases

### Act 1: The Underdog (Rifter)
- **Complaint:** "Pile of rust duct-taped together"
- **Victory:** "In Rust We Trust!"
- **Theme:** Making do with what you have, pride in survival

### Act 2: The Proven (Wolf)
- **Recognition:** "No more rust - just steel!"
- **Victory:** "The Wolf hunts well!"
- **Theme:** Earned respect, growing confidence

### Act 3: The Legend (Jaguar)
- **Realization:** "From rust to legend!"
- **Victory:** "Lightning strike!"
- **Theme:** Full circle, remembering humble beginnings

## Voice Direction Notes

### Ace Pilot Voice
- Age: Mid-20s to early 30s
- Accent: Neutral or slight Icelandic tint (EVE connection)
- Tone: Confident but not arrogant
- Delivery styles:
  - **Complaint:** Sarcastic, disbelieving, slight whine
  - **Victory (Good):** Triumphant, energetic, proud
  - **Victory (Struggling):** Exhausted but relieved
  - **Reflection:** Thoughtful, nostalgic (later game)

### Elder Voices (by Tribe)
- **Sebiestor:** Measured, analytical, slightly apologetic
- **Brutor:** Gruff, challenging, warrior-like
- **Vherokior:** Calm, mysterious, wise
- **Krusual:** Flat, pragmatic, tactical
