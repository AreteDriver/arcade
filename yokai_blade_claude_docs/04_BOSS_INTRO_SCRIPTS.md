# YOKAI BLADE — Boss Intro Scripts (Diegetic, Skippable)

Short, sincere, and repeatable. The world never winks.

## Tier 1 — Teaching

### Kasa-Obake
**Scene:** A lone umbrella bounces into view. One eye opens.  
**Action:** It spins too fast, trips, straightens proudly.  
**Line:** “Still standing… still dancing.”  
**Music:** Light percussion begins.

### Chōchin-Obake
**Scene:** A lantern sways. Flame flickers.  
**Action:** Room brightens—then dims. Blue ground-flames appear.  
**Line:** “Light is borrowed. Fire is not.”  
**Music:** Low hum + crackle.

### Hitotsume-Kozō
**Scene:** Bare feet slap the ground. Laughter echoes.  
**Action:** He darts past, stops just out of reach, grins.  
**Line:** “Catch me first.”  
**Music:** Fast mischievous tempo.

## Tier 2 — Skill & Pressure

### Rokurokubi
**Scene:** A woman stands perfectly still, back turned.  
**Action:** Neck stretches slowly, silently.  
**Line:** “Do not stare. I am still here.”  
**Music:** String tension begins.

### Ittan-Momen
**Scene:** Cloth flutters gently.  
**Action:** It tightens around the edges; space shrinks.  
**Line:** “Cut carefully.”  
**Music:** Tightening breath-rhythm.

### Dorotabō
**Scene:** Water ripples. Mud bubbles.  
**Action:** A hand breaks the surface. Then a face.  
**Line:** “You left it to rot.”  
**Music:** Heavy dragging bass + distant wails.

### Nuppeppō
**Scene:** A mass of flesh slumps motionless.  
**Action:** It exhales; the air turns thick.  
**Line:** “Time feeds me.”  
**Music:** Oppressive drone.

### Teke-Teke
**Scene:** Silence.  
**Action:** Scraping sound approaches.  
**Line:** “Hear me?”  
**Music:** None—audio carries the fight.

## Tier 3 — Mastery & Absurdity

### Jorōgumo
**Scene:** A woman plays a biwa.  
**Action:** The final note hangs too long; threads glint.  
**Line:** “Come closer.”  
**Music:** Biwa fractures into combat rhythm.

### Nue
**Scene:** Shadow moves behind mist. Shapes overlap.  
**Action:** Eyes open—too many, misaligned.  
**Line:** “Choose quickly.”  
**Music:** Tempo shifts.

### Gashadokuro
**Scene:** Teeth chatter in the distance.  
**Action:** Giant skull rises, blotting out moon.  
**Line:** “Climb.”  
**Music:** Vast war drums.

### Baku
**Scene:** Colors bleed; the world softens.  
**Action:** The Baku’s shadow lags behind.  
**Line:** “Sleep.”  
**Music:** Warped echoes of earlier themes.

### Shirime
**Scene:** A man stands politely on the road, back turned.  
**Action:** He bows. Waits.  
**Line:** “May I show you something?”  
**Music:** None until player commits.

### Tanuki
**Scene:** A familiar silhouette approaches.  
**Action:** Flicker—tail visible for a split second.  
**Line:** “You’re certain?”  
**Music:** Playful rhythm, no parody.

## Tier 4 — Divine

### Raijin & Fūjin
**Scene:** Temple gates slam open.  
**Action:** Thunder cracks. Wind howls from opposite sides.  
**Raijin:** “Stand.”  
**Fūjin:** “Endure.”  
**Music:** Dual rhythms collide.

### Kyūbi no Kitsune
**Scene:** Court lady kneels, serene.  
**Action:** Shadow shows nine tails.  
**Line:** “You may still kneel.”  
**Music:** Court music turns predatory.

### Oni
**Scene:** Gates of Jigoku loom behind him.  
**Action:** He plants kanabō and waits.  
**Line:** “Begin.”  
**Music:** Single drumbeat motif.

## Final Trial

### Yamata no Orochi
**Scene:** Eight valleys. Eight heads rise in silence.  
**Action:** Each head watches a different direction.  
**Line:** “Choose.” (layered, overlapping)  
**Music:** Themes woven together.

## Secret

### Amenominakanushi
**Scene:** White emptiness.  
**Action:** Form shifts, never settling.  
**Line:** “You already know.” (felt, not spoken)  
**Music:** None.
