# YOKAI BLADE — Boss Proverbs (One-Line Folklore Truths)

Use as shrine inscriptions, loading lines, or whispered diegetic prompts.
These are **truths**, not tutorials.

## Tier 1 — Teaching

- **Kasa-Obake**: “Even the foolish reveal their rhythm if you watch them long enough.”
- **Chōchin-Obake**: “Light shows the path; fire decides where you may walk.”
- **Hitotsume-Kozō**: “The timid are never caught, but they are never finished.”

## Tier 2 — Skill & Pressure

- **Rokurokubi**: “The head may roam, but the body never flees.”
- **Ittan-Momen**: “A careless cut creates two enemies.”
- **Dorotabō**: “Those who rush through sorrow drown in it.”
- **Nuppeppō**: “Rot waits patiently for those who hesitate.”
- **Teke-Teke**: “The ears must see before the eyes can move.”

## Tier 3 — Mastery & Absurdity

- **Jorōgumo**: “Beauty distracts; threads decide.”
- **Nue**: “Know what you face, or be crushed by what you guessed.”
- **Gashadokuro**: “Giants fall only to those willing to climb.”
- **Baku**: “Dreams lie with color; truth casts a shadow.”
- **Shirime**: “Not every threat demands a blade.”
- **Tanuki**: “Certainty is the easiest shape to imitate.”

## Tier 4 — Divine

- **Raijin & Fūjin**: “Storms are never faced one at a time.”
- **Kyūbi no Kitsune**: “What is severed returns unless purified.”
- **Oni**: “When tricks end, skill remains.”

## Final Trial

- **Yamata no Orochi**: “Order decides whether chaos devours or obeys.”

## Secret

- **Amenominakanushi**: “Before pattern, there was knowing.”
